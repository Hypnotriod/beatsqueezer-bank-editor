Add Scene Builder to Netbeans 8.0.2

You can download JavaFX Scene Builder 8.2
 http://gluonhq.com/labs/scene-builder/
 http://www.oracle.com/technetwork/java/javafxscenebuilder-1x-archive-2199384.html
Then to link it to Netbeans IDE 8.0.2 go to Tools>Options>Java>JavaFX
Select Browse from the Scene Builder Home drop down menu
Finally navigate to the previously downloaded JavaFX Scene Builder 2.0 and click Open and then Ok
